Kerala / The Leela

From its cliff top setting the Leela commands panoramic views of the Kovalam coastline. Just steps away from a sandy beach, it offers a range of stylish accommodation – the Club rooms featuring a residents-only sea view wing, with private check-in and numerous exclusive services. Dining options encompass European, Asian and Indian at the Poolside restaurant, while on the beachfront Tides restaurant has a dance floor and live bands. Two infinity pools overlook the Arabian Sea; a wellness spa at the hotel’s highest point features treatments using medicinal plants from the spa greenhouse.

Udaipur / OBEROI UDAIVILAS

Set on the banks of Lake Pichola and designed in the style of a golden-domed Rajasthani palace, Udaivilas captures the romance and splendour of a bygone era. Many of its richly decorated rooms give uninterrupted views of the old city of Udaipur, some with semi-private infinity edge pools with lake view. Stroll through the landscaped gardens, relax with a drink or submit yourself to soothing aromatherapies at the spa. The main dining room serves international and Indian cuisine, whilst the speciality Indian restaurant serves traditional Rajasthani food. Nearby Udaipur is known for its palatial architecture and colourful bazaars.

Jaipur / RAMBAGH PALACE

Once the opulent former residence of the Maharaja of Jaipur. Elegant accommodation includes exquisitely designed Historical Suites and sumptuous Royal Suites, once royal chambers. Dining at majestic Suvarna Mahal will transport you back to the golden era of Rajasthan, whilst the congenial Polo Bar with its display of trophies won by the late Maharaja is rated amongst the finest bars in the world. Recreations contained within the vast grounds encompass indoor and outdoor swimming pools, croquet, Indian therapies and a fitness centre.
